Semantic Element Tags

Following semantic tags are used to accurately describe the kind of content that is rendered on the page. div and p does not communicate much, so tags such as section and aside are better at conveying the browser what the content/the purpose of the content is.

These tags do not have any styling of their own, and thus need to be styled.

header tag: for headings
nav tag: for navigation bar at the top
section tag: actual content
article tag: additional content
aside tag: unrelated content on the side such as links to other posts
footer tag: for information at the bottom of the page, eg copyright information
